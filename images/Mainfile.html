About Us

Wings Jeevan Ki Ek Nai Udaan is 100% voluntary organization. Our organization has only one objective "Awareness in all".
For this purpose, we are spreading awareness among all for blood donation, health, education, women empowerment, livelihood, environment, water conservation, food wastage management.

Upcoming events

Our history

With the aim of meeting the shortage of blood donation, we started our voluntary service in the name of "Need Blood Jhansi" WhatsApp group and then we registered the organization as name "Wings Jeevan Ki Ek Nai Udaan" in 20 Dec 2018.

Mission

For this purpose, we are spreading awareness among all for blood donation, health, education, women empowerment, livelihood, environment, water conservation, food wastage management.
